What is BeautyMe ?

BeautyMe is a product which has utilized exemplary techniques in Deep Learning and Computer Vision provided in literature with regard to Virtual Makeover and Recommendation. Choosing the perfect makeup that best suits a person is challenging unless they have years of expertise with cosmetics or are specialized in understanding the makeup industry. The proposed Web Application enables users to apply virtual makeup in an augmented reality environment while being provided makeup recommendations. Makeup Recommendations are generated for the makeup products namely, lipstick, eyeshadow and foundation considering userâ€™s skin tone, skin type, hair colour, colour and type of clothing, occasion and the time user would use makeup.

Research Gap

The current systems which provide virtual makeover capabilities have not considered recommendation of makeup products based on the user specific traits. Most systems are still on research level and have not been integrated for commercial use. BeautyMe application enables recommendation of makeup products with least user inputs by automatically detecting skin tone, skin type, hair colour, and type and colour of the clothing of the user. The makeup recommended is being simulated real-time. The user is given the choice of selecting the colour and texture of makeup products which would then be simulated real-time on the video stream captured preserving the texture and colour of the skin.

Research problem

Usage of makeup products is a common technique to enhance beauty and to create a new look that would uplift personal character. In the present, different makeup styles are used based on the type of occasion a person may attend. To mitigate the problems of removing and reapplying makeup on the face when deciding on a makeup style, the concept of virtual makeover has been sprouting over the past few years. Based on the visualization of makeup on the userâ€™s virtual face, the user would develop either fondness or dislike towards the makeup product. This has a substantial impact on purchasing makeup products online. If the user is given appropriate suggestions based on their considerations, much productivity is attained and would increase profits on e-commerce platforms. Even though existing systems facilitate the simulation of selected makeup on the userâ€™s face, they do not provide any makeup recommendations despite virtual makeover systems are to be designed to aid in the decision-making process of choosing the right makeup style for a certain person.

Research objective

  • Main Objective

    • Development of a web application capable of real-time makeup simulation which provide makeup recommendations to users based on hair colour, skin tone, skin type, colour of clothing, type of clothing, time and occasion user plans on wearing makeup.

  • Specific Objectives

    • Realtime facial landmark detection and makeup simulation including sideview of the face.

    • Skin tone, skin type and hair colour detection

    • Clothing type and clothing colour detection, and clothing colour classification

    • Providing personalized recommendations on makeup products such as foundation, lipstick, and eye shadow considering a userâ€™s personal traits, history of usage and external attributes.

Methodology

To eliminate the need to be concerned about the appearance after applying makeup since it provides users with real-time makeup simulation and makeup recommendations. The proposed system enables real-time makeup simulation in an Augmented Reality (AR) environment and recommends makeup styles considering skin tone, hair colour, colour of clothing, type of clothing and type of occasion to be attended with makeup. Skin tone, hair colour, and colour and type of clothing of a user would be automatically detected and processed to generate recommendations for makeup products, namely lipstick, foundation and eyeshadow. Complications of wasting makeup products and time, and cleaning makeup can be mitigated by using a real-time makeup simulation system. Recommendations generated by the application assist the users to decide on makeup styles and provides a better user experience.

Technologies

Deep Learning algorithms with image processing techniques are used to create the Web Application.

  • PyTorch, TensorFlow: model training

  • OpenCV, Pillow, scipy python libraries: image processing

  • Django: server-side development of the webapplication

  • React JS: UI development

  • MongoDB Atlas: cloud database
